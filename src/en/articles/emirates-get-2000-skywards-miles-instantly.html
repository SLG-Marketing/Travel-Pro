Chauffeur Drive Service

At Emirates, we do not compromise when it comes to serve our First and Business Class guests in the correct manner.

Emirates offers a FREE Chauffeur-drive service to all premium travellers and will arrange their pick-up from their homes/offices to the airport and when they land we’ll be there to take them to their final destination in over 70 cities worldwide.

Who is eligible?

Any First Class or Business Class traveller is eligible even when booking a promotional ticket, however please note that our H-class doesn't include a free Chauffeur Drive Service.

The Chauffeur-drive service is also FREE all the way to / from Brussels Airport for First and Business Class passengers living or working in the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg.

The Complimentary Chauffeur-drive service is totally free in a radius of 70 KM around Brussels Airport. Beyond the 70 KM, passengers are required to pay EUR 4 per KM.

Any excess mileage invoices will be sent to the email address in the booking. Payments shall be made online directly to the service provider through the link on the email using Visa, Mastercard or American Express cards and cannot be made to the driver directly.

How to book the Chauffeur-drive service?

  • Through your GDS system
  • Call our Reservation Help desk: 02/700.70.07
  • Via the ‘Manage existing booking’ section on our website

Arrange your Chauffeur drive pick up at least 12 hours ahead of your flight. You can add it to your itinerary when you book your flight, or later through Manage Your Booking. When you land, check in with your driver at our Chauffeur drive desks.

Those interested can sign up via emirates.com, flydubai.com, through the official Emirates app, or the flydubai app and directly from the Wi-Fi portal onboard.

Platinum Skywards members have complimentary internet access in all classes.
