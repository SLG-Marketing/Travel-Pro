More information

Travel Pro has been part of the family-run SLG group founded in 1948. The group is one of the market leaders in the mobility and travel sectors in Luxembourg and the Greater Region.

Values

Travel Pro's corporate values are based on the values of our parent company SLG. They form the foundation of our actions and represent what is important to us and what drives us in everything we do.

Employees

 

We support our employees by advocating absolute equality. We reject any form of discrimination based on gender, origin, religion, age or sexual orientation.

Data protection & compliance commitment

 

Transparency, data security, personal rights, data retention, training and awareness-raising on these topics as well as close cooperation with the authorities are of great importance to us.

Health and safety

 

The health of our employees and the safety of our customers are always our top priority.

Innovation

 

Our goal is to make travel booking and travel journeys easier for our clients. We always strive to offer innovative solutions and services and try to be at the forefront of technology in our industry.

Supplier partnership

 

Travel Pro treats its suppliers as important allies. We select them according to quality criteria and give preference to those who have a similar corporate culture to ours.

Our approach to competition

 

We respect the property of our market participants and reject any illegal appropriation of business information as well as any questionable actions for competitive advantage.

Environmental responsibility

 

At Travel Pro, we are committed to reducing our environmental footprint as much as possible. We prioritize sustainability in all our business processes and strive to partner with like-minded companies who share our ambitions. Together, we can work towards a more environmentally responsible travel industry.

Social commitment

 

As an employer committed to social responsibility, we recognize our responsibility to integrate and secure jobs within our organization. Additionally, we are dedicated to actively promoting more responsible, sustainable, and respectful tourism.

Corporate governance

 

We are part of a trusted group of companies that values stable, long-term management. The management's actions are based on professionalism, responsiveness, and efficiency. SLG's goal is to find innovative solutions that make customers' lives easier, at Travel Pro we strive to do the same.
